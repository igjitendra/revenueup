Most businesses do not have an activity problem. They have a **connection problem**.

Content creates attention, but the offer does not convert it. Ads generate traffic, but the landing page does not establish trust.

## Start with the business constraint

Before choosing a channel, identify the constraint:

- Is the offer difficult to understand?
- Is trust too low for the buying decision?
- Is the conversion journey creating friction?
- Is measurement too weak to guide decisions?

## Build one connected system

1. **Attention** — distinctive ideas and distribution.
2. **Trust** — proof, expertise and a credible experience.
3. **Action** — a clear offer and an easy next step.

> Strong growth comes from clearer decisions, not more random activity.
